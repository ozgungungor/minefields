Minesweeper Legends

A modern take on the classic minefield puzzle.

About

Minesweeper Legends is a strategic update to the classic puzzle game. It adds a health and energy system, special abilities such as Sonar and Heal, multiple themes, and a custom board mode. The app is built with SwiftUI for iOS.
